My Slambook

A slambook is a notebook or journal passed aroung among friends and classmates where everyone writes personal information, answers fun questions, and shares thoughts or messages. It usually includes questions like name, birthday, favorites, dreams, and messages to owner. Popular in schools, slambooks are a fun way to learn more about each other and keep memories.
