Megan's Top 3 Harry Potter Books

Harry Potter is an incredible series that captures your imagination from the very first page. Its magical world, relatable characters, and thrilling adventures make reading feel like an exciting journey rather than a chore. For many, it’s the perfect gateway into the joy of reading because it combines mystery, friendship, and fantasy in a way that’s impossible to put down. Once you dive into Harry’s story, you’re hooked and eager to explore even more books.

Goblet of fire

Why I Like It:

  1. The thrilling Triwizard Tournament challenges the champions
  2. The return of Voldemort raises the stakes dramatically
  3. The Yule Ball adds magical excitement and teenage drama.

Prisoner of Azkaban

Why I Like It:

  1. The time-turner creates a clever and suspenseful plot twist
  2. The darker, more mature tone deepens the story’s complexity
  3. The Introduction of the Marauders added much more to the story

The Half-Blood Prince

Why I Like It:

  1. The exploration of Voldemort’s past reveals crucial secrets
  2. Harry’s discovery of the Half-Blood Prince’s potion book adds mystery
  3. The emotional endingmarks a significant turning point in the series
